Uganda

Uganda, located in East Africa, is known for its diverse wildlife, including mountain gorillas, and its rich cultural heritage. The country boasts stunning landscapes, from the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains to the savannahs of the Queen Elizabeth National Park. Uganda is also home to Lake Victoria, the largest lake in Africa.
